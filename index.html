Welcome, Educators!

Ice Age Trail · Geology · Classrooms

This map was built for teachers who want to take students onto the Ice Age Trail and feel confident talking about what they see. You do not need to be a geologist – the goal is to give you just enough background to connect the landscape to what students are learning in class.

How to use this map

  • Explore the trail: Zoom to your region and click an orange trail segment to see an overview written for teachers.
  • Dig into the geology: Turn on the bedrock and glacial layers, and use the Geologic Concepts tab to connect landforms to classroom ideas.
  • Use it for teaching: Move between the Segments, Geologic Concepts, and Activities tabs to prep lessons before, during, or after a field trip.
  • Extend learning: Click the light blue dots to explore geologic points of interest, and open Educator Resources for standards and printable guides.
